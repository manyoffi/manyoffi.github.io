Meet MANY, the 19-year-old hip-hop prodigy hailing from Kherson, Ukraine. Inspired by his surroundings and personal experiences, MANY infuses Ukrainian folk music with modern rap beats, creating a unique and captivating sound.

With a gift for crafting poignant verses and infectious hooks, MANY quickly gained attention within the local music scene, earning him a dedicated following and accolades from his peers. Now, poised for stardom, he prepares to release his debut album and embark on a nationwide tour, ready to share his talent and passion with the world.
